Kahinju Secondary School is located along Fort-Portal, Bundibugyo Road, 2km from Fort-Portal Tourism City in Western Uganda.

Kahinju Secondary School is registered as a government Aided School by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MoES) with the EMIS Number of 118020.
